About this ePortfolio

Hi! I am Ivan James Lo, a first-year college student taking up BS Computer Science at Ateneo de Davao University. I am 19 years old and turning 20 in a few days. I reside in Bangkal, Davao City, and I belong to Sir Francis Lloyd Singson's class with the class code 16-411.

For context, I'm not so much of a physically active person, but I am not also idle. Because of this, I find the journey I have been through in this course (PE 1114) to be physically challenging but rewarding. This course slapped me with the reality that I need to do more physical activity and exercises because I've noticed that I easily get tired and drained after doing some exercises. Moreover, I really had a hard time doing push-ups (which you will see in the Pre-Test and Post-Test of the Movement Competency Screen). Thus, I ask for your kind consideration and judgment when browsing this website.

On the brighter side, I got to know my limits and improved some aspects of my physical fitness. The improvement may not be drastic, but it is still an improvement. Some of the skills and knowledge I learned from the activities done in this course are the following. First, how to properly warm up and cool down my body and the importance of these. Second, how to properly assess my body's strengths and weaknesses and enhance those identified weaknesses. Last but not least, appreciate the importance of doing physical exercises daily. These skills and knowledge will help me pursue a life-long fitness journey as these will motivate me to keep exercising and remind myself that being physically active is essential if I want to live a longer and healthier life.

Preliminaries

Before moving forward, we should be reminded of some of the do's and don'ts of exercise to prevent injuries and accidents. Also, these will be your guide to whether you are fit to exercise for the day or not. Exercise is essential, but if you don't know the do's and don'ts, you might suffer in the end.

Here are some of the Do's and Don'ts:

Do's Don'ts
Do exercise when you're in good condition. Don't engagae in physical activity / exercise when you are not feeling well.
Do 30-40 minutes workout twice or thrice a week. Don't overlook yourself while exercising.
Do ensure that you have covered all body areas for your exercise. Don't always focus on a single form of training.
Do eat enough food and drinkk enough liquids to avoid passing out. Don't eat heavy meals or drink too many liquids before exercising.
Do make exercise a habit. Don't be too obsessed with exercise.
